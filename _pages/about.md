### Jingkun (Allen) Liu

I graduated from Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in 2022 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science. After graduating, I worked as an Embedded Robotics Software Engineer at DEKA Research & Development, where I contributed to cutting-edge medical and mobility devices, developing real-time control systems and hardware-software integration solutions.

My passion for building intelligent robotic systems led me to pursue a Master's in Robotics at Northwestern University. While at Northwestern, I worked extensively with robotic manipulation, SLAM, autonomous navigation, computer vision, and machine learning. I graduated with my M.S. in Robotics in 2025.

Since graduating, I've been serving as the Head of Robotic Engineering at Whelix, where I lead the development of next-generation robotic solutions. My work spans embedded systems, robot manipulation, motion planning, and machine learning for robotics.

I thrive at the intersection of mechanical design, electrical systems, and intelligent software, building robots that can perceive, reason, and act in complex real-world environments.

**Core Expertise:**
- **Embedded Systems**: Real-time control systems, microcontroller programming, hardware-software integration
- **Robot Manipulation**: Motion planning, kinematics, trajectory optimization, and control
- **Machine Learning for Robotics**: Computer vision, reinforcement learning, and perception systems
- **Software Development**: ROS/ROS2, C/C++, Python, with a focus on robust and scalable architectures

### 刘景堃 (Allen)

我于2022年毕业于罗斯-霍尔曼理工学院，获得机械工程和计算机科学学士学位。毕业后，我在DEKA研究与开发公司担任嵌入式机器人软件工程师，参与开发尖端医疗和移动设备，负责实时控制系统和软硬件集成解决方案的开发。

我对构建智能机器人系统的热情促使我在西北大学攻读机器人学硕士学位。在西北大学期间，我深入研究了机器人操作、SLAM、自主导航、计算机视觉和机器学习。我于2025年获得机器人学硕士学位。

毕业后，我一直担任Whelix的机器人工程主管，领导下一代机器人解决方案的开发。我的工作涵盖嵌入式系统、机器人操作、运动规划和机器人机器学习。

我热衷于机械设计、电气系统和智能软件的交叉领域，构建能够在复杂现实环境中感知、推理和行动的机器人。

**核心专长：**
- **嵌入式系统**：实时控制系统、微控制器编程、软硬件集成
- **机器人操作**：运动规划、运动学、轨迹优化和控制
- **机器人机器学习**：计算机视觉、强化学习和感知系统
- **软件开发**：ROS/ROS2、C/C++、Python，专注于稳健可扩展的架构
